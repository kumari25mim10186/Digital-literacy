#     DO’S AND DON’TS 
## WHAT TO DO 
* Give more than you take 
* Show your unpolished side 
* Read the room  
* Cheer for others 
* Listen to the crowd 
## WHAT NOT TO DO  
* Don't buy fake friends 
* Don't wrestle with trolls 
* Don't hide from real mistakes 
* Don't ghost your audience 
* Don't force an opinion
