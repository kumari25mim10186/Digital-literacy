# social media prevention checklist
## The Do's
* Use a Password Manager to generate long, unique passwords. 
* Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) everywhere. 
* Hover over links to check the URL before clicking. 
* Use a VPN if you have to connect to public Wi-Fi. 
* Hang up and call the oAicial organization directly to verify. 
## The Don'ts 
* Don't Share Sensitive Info Unprompted. 
* Download attachments from unknown or unexpected senders. 
* Don't Let Strangers Access Your Devices. 
* Check your bank account on an unsecured coAee shop network. 
* Trust unsolicited callers claiming to be police, tech support, or your bank. 
## UPI & Financial Safety Tips 
* Fraudsters send request-to-pay notifications.  
* Approving them transfers money from your account, not to you.  
* Always verify the sender before approving 
* Never Share Your UPI PIN
